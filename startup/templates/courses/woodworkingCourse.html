Wordworking with hand tools

Instructor: Brian Shourd

Description: In this course, I'll teach you to make wooden objects the way that nature intended - using the power of steel and Snickers bars. Working only by hand, I'll teach you all the techniques that you need to learn to care for, set up, and of course use your tools, from sharpening to sawing. By the end, you'll have finished your very own small project - a simple decorative jewelry box, and you'll be well on your way to mastering this delightful hobby.

Start Date: April 6th, 2013

End Date: April 12th, 2013

Lesson 1: Sharpening

Basic coverage of common hand tools, how to care for them, and safety procedures.

Lesson 2: Saws

All about saws. Learn to use backsaws and panel saws to dimension stock.

Lesson 3: Final Dimensioning with Planes

Learn how to use the jointer plane and the jack plane to bring to stock to final dimensions.
